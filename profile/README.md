## 🚀 Overview
NinjaTrader for Windows brings charting, market analysis, strategy development, and order execution together in a single desktop workspace geared toward futures and forex traders. Its defining strength is a flexible charting engine paired with NinjaTrader indicators that you can configure, combine, or extend with your own code. Traders use it to study price action, test ideas on historical data before committing real capital, and place orders directly from the chart. The add-ons ecosystem lets you layer additional tools onto the core platform as your workflow grows.

## ✨ Features
- **Advanced charting** — Study multiple markets and timeframes with a fast, configurable charting engine built for detailed price analysis.
- **Customizable indicators** — Adjust the built-in NinjaTrader indicators or write your own to match the way you read the market.
- **Strategy backtesting** — Test trading ideas against historical data to review how a rule set would have behaved before going live.
- **Order execution** — Place, modify, and manage orders directly from charts and order-entry tools for futures and forex.
- **Add-ons ecosystem** — Extend the platform with third-party and custom add-ons, from additional indicators to full analysis packages.

## ❓ FAQ
**Is NinjaTrader free?**
NinjaTrader is free to use for advanced charting, strategy backtesting, and simulated (paper) trading. Live trading is available through paid options, offered as a lease or a lifetime license, and additional data or brokerage fees may apply depending on your setup.

**Which versions of Windows are supported?**
NinjaTrader runs on modern 64-bit versions of Windows, including Windows 10 and Windows 11. Keeping Windows updated helps the platform run smoothly, especially when you have several charts and indicators open at once.

**Is NinjaTrader legit?**
Yes. NinjaTrader is an established, legitimate trading platform and brokerage that has been used by futures and forex traders for many years. As with any market activity, trading involves risk, so review the platform's own materials and your own risk tolerance before trading live.

**How does NinjaTrader compare to Tradovate?**
Both NinjaTrader and Tradovate are futures-focused platforms. NinjaTrader is a desktop application known for its customizable indicators, backtesting, and add-ons ecosystem, while Tradovate is a cloud- and browser-oriented platform. They use different fee models and tooling, so the right choice depends on your workflow and whether you prefer a desktop or web-based environment.

**Can I add my own indicators?**
Yes. Beyond the popular indicators that ship with the platform, you can customize existing NinjaTrader indicators or build your own, and you can install additional indicators and add-ons from the wider ecosystem.
